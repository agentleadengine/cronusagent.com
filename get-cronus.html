How to get Cronus

Cronus is in private alpha, available as part of Agent Lead Engine LLC's consulting work. That is not a paywall for a download button - it is a person setting your stack up properly, on machines you own.

What the engagement looks like

  • Installed on your machines. We set up Cronus on your computer (Mac, Windows, or Linux), not on our servers. From day one, everything it holds is yours.
  • Configured for your work. Models chosen for your budget (free local, flat-rate, or frontier), your tools connected, your agents wired in, the memory system running.
  • You, trained. A working session where you learn to route work, use agent windows, weave context in Orchestra, and read the health check - so the tool does not depend on us.
  • Alpha support. Direct line while you are early. Your feedback shapes what gets built next, and updates arrive as installers you choose to run.

Who this is for

People who already use AI seriously and are tired of running it as a pile of disconnected tabs and terminals: consultants, small business owners, operators, and builders. If you use one chatbot occasionally, you do not need this yet - and we will tell you so.

What it costs

Engagements are scoped to what your setup actually needs, so there is no price sticker on this page. Email us, tell us what your stack looks like today, and you will get a straight answer - including "you do not need us for this" when that is the truth.

The ownership guarantee

Everything Cronus builds up for you - memory, skills, automations, history - lives in files on your machines. If we part ways, all of it stays with you. We think that should be table stakes for any AI vendor; we make it the deal explicitly. (More on this in you own it.)

Start the conversation

Email agentleadengine@gmail.com with two or three sentences: who you are, what AI tools you use today, and what you wish worked better. That is the whole application.
