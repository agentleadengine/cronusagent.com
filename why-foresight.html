Guides · The why

Foresight: your time back

Hindsight remembers what you do. Foresight reads those memories, finds the work you keep doing by hand, and proposes the automation - with time math that states its own basis.

47 seconds · narrated · sound on

How it keeps itself honest

  • Evidence or silence. Every proposal quotes the memories it is based on. If your history does not support a pattern, Foresight returns nothing - and says so.
  • Estimates labeled as estimates. "Roughly 2 hours a week" always comes with its basis ("appears in 9 memories across June and July"), never dressed up as a measurement.
  • You stay the decision. "Hand to Cronus" writes the build request and puts it in your chat. Nothing is built until you read it and press send.
  • Dismissed stays dismissed. Ideas you reject do not come back on the next scan.
The point

Not automation for its own sake - getting back the hours you never noticed you were spending. Cronus is the one tool on your desk that offers to shorten its own to-do list.
