Guides · The why

You own all of it

The most important thing about Cronus is not a feature. Sixty-nine seconds on ownership: your memory, your skills, your models, on hardware you control.

69 seconds · narrated · sound on

The promise, in text

  • Your memory is a folder on your machine. Copy it, back it up, delete it. No company holds the record of your work.
  • Your skills travel with you. What your agents learn is files on your disk, not features of someone's subscription.
  • Models are interchangeable parts. Banned, priced out, or provider gone? Pick a different brain, including free local ones. Everything else stays.
  • There is no Cronus account. No login, no cloud, no telemetry - so there is no platform that can take your work away.
The one-sentence case

You already own the agents; Cronus gives them one desk, one memory, and one honest dashboard, on hardware you control.
