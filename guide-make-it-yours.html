Make it yours

Cronus is your desk. This guide is the comfort pass: make it look like yours, and learn the two fastest moves in the app.

narrated · sound on

Why this matters

Comfort is a feature

You will spend hours a week at this desk. A look you chose, a palette that jumps anywhere in a keystroke, and an @-mention that skips the router - each saves seconds that compound into wanting to be here.

The walkthrough

The style button

Top right. It reskins the entire app: font pack, accent color, corner sharpness, spacing density, and glow. Pick, save, done - every page follows instantly, and nothing but looks changes.

Cmd+K: jump anywhere

The search bar up top (or Command K anywhere - Control K on Windows) opens the jump palette. Type a screen's name and go. Every entry carries a plain-words description, so the palette doubles as a map of the whole app.

@-mentions: skip the router from chat

In the main composer, start a message with @hermes, @claude, or @openclaw and it goes straight to that agent - no routing, no window needed. The fastest direct line in the app.

The ? button

Every control in Cronus has a plain-language tooltip, and the ? in the top bar explains how the big pieces fit. When in doubt, hover.
