Powerful tools, real consequences

Cronus is built around safety - checked prompts, controlled context, visible actions (see our Mission) - but no design removes your responsibility. Agents you run can send messages, modify files, and act on real accounts using access you give them. Grant access thoughtfully, review what agents do, keep backups of anything important, and never give an agent credentials or permissions you would not give a new employee on their first day.

Privacy, concretely

Cronus has no account system, no analytics, and no telemetry. We do not know you installed it. Your conversations, memories, credentials, and files stay on your machine in folders you can open and delete. The only data that leaves your computer is a message you choose to send to a model provider you configured (for example Anthropic, Google, or OpenRouter), which is then governed by that provider's privacy terms, not ours. The credential vault stores keys encrypted, and this website sets no tracking cookies.
