What none of this can do

Watch: the honest ending · 52 seconds · animated, sound on

The honest ending

No context system, no memory, no grader, no guardrail gets hallucinations to zero. Anyone who tells you otherwise is selling something.

What the stack actually does

It shrinks the problem. Deliberate context and grounded memory mean less guessing. Graded prompts mean better inputs. Approvals mean risky actions get caught by a human before they happen. The result is smaller, rarer, more visible mistakes. Not zero mistakes.

The last check is yours

Check work that matters before you rely on it. Every honest AI company will tell you the same thing, usually in the fine print. We put it in the headline: Cronus is built to make checking easy, not to make it unnecessary.

The formal version

Cronus is alpha software from Agent Lead Engine LLC. AI models can be wrong, and you are responsible for verifying your own work before acting on it. The disclaimer is the complete statement, and Security, in plain words covers the practical risks of running agents at all.
