Changelog

What shipped and when, in plain words. The app never updates itself: it checks this site only when you click "Check for updates", and installing a new version is always your call.

0.2.1July 21, 2026current
  • Composer @-mentions: start a message with @hermes, @claude, or @openclaw to skip the router and talk straight to that agent.
  • Prompt Rater on the composer: strict A to F grades, judged before you send, with reasons that explain themselves.
  • Fix it for me: an A-standard rewrite of your prompt that you approve before it sends.
  • Drafts survive restarts.
  • New look across the whole app: layered dark surfaces, one gold accent, icons in the navigation, and a proper welcome screen. Same features, easier on the eyes.
  • Security fixes from a full audit of the codebase, plus two repaired pages (conversation Log and Files) that broke during an earlier cleanup.
  • All seventeen site videos re-recorded on the new design.
0.2.0July 20, 2026
  • First locked installers: signed-code-free downloads for macOS, Windows, and Linux, protected by your alpha access code.
  • cronusagent.com launched, with the guide video series and written docs.
  • Foresight: reads your agents' memory for repeated manual work and proposes automations, with honest confidence labels.
  • Usage budget: a monthly dollar cap where only real per-token spend counts.
  • Tool Health, Safety audits, and the approvals queue in one Watch section.
Earlier alphasJuly 2026
  • The desktop app itself: an Electron shell that starts the local backend and opens the dashboard as a real application.
  • Oracle chat with honest routing, the agent dock and windows, Orchestra, Files, Memory on Hindsight, Local Models through Ollama, the setup wizard, and the credential vault.

Running an older version? Open Updates inside the app and click "Check for updates", or grab the latest installer from Get Cronus.
